[About Our Studio]

Vocal Recording

Vocals are recorded in a soundproof booth using a high-end condenser microphone and outboard signal chain, giving you clean, detailed takes. The booth includes a mirrored monitor display so vocalists stay connected to the session.

  • AKG C414 Microphone
  • Rupert Neve 511 Preamp
  • dbx 560A Compressor/Limiter

Guitars & Amps

Track electric guitars through real amps or direct setups, with flexible options for tone shaping. Acoustic guitars are captured using a dual-mic approach for clarity and depth.

Synths, Keys & MIDI

A collection of analog and digital synths, weighted keys, and a full MIDI routing system lets you control multiple instruments at once inside your DAW.

Drum Production

Electronic drums tracked via MIDI allow precise control over feel and sound, paired with a wide selection of professional drum libraries.

Modular Workflow

Bring your own laptop, interface, or preferred setup — or plug directly into ours. The studio is built to adapt to how you work.
